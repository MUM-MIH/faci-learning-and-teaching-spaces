Learning & Teaching Spaces

This topic covers the basic skills required to operate the learning and teaching spaces in Monash to its fullest extent. Each learning and teaching space is equipped with various technologies that can help both the tutor and the student communicate better. The understanding on how to efficiently operate the various technologies in a given space will greatly promote improved student experience, attention and attainment.

Topic Objectives:

  1. Explains basic functions and capabilities of each learning and teaching space.
  2. Demonstrates fundamental user skills required to operate the various technologies in the learning and teaching space
  3. Provides 'best practice' examples of how to use the technologies available in a learning and teaching space to deliver the best mode of teaching to the students
