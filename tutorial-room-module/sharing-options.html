[fa-filter fa-lg] Alternate Sharing Options

Academics who prefer to use their own devices to conduct lessons need not worry about compatibility issues. The Tutorial Rooms are equipped with a BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) sharing option which will be detailed in the following video.

Follow the instructions in the video to access the alternative sharing options.

  1. Identify the VGA display cable and the 3.5mm audio jack.
  2. Connect the VGA display cable to the VGA port of your computer.
  3. Connect the 3.5mm audio jack to your device.
  4. Turn on the Video Switch. Select channel 2 as the desired input. The feed from your device is now routed to the projector.
  5. Click the keys on your device to open the windows projection menu. Then select from the four options presented to you.


[fa-exclamation-circle fa-lg] What if I don't have a VGA port?

  • Ensure that the appropriate VGA adapter for your device is available. Most devices are equipped with either a HDMI or a Type C port.
  • If your device is equipped with a HDMI port, a HDMI to VGA adapter will be required. Connect the VGA display cable to the HDMI adapter securely.
  • Connect the adapter to your device.
