[fa-audio-description fa-lg] Audio & Visual Equipment

To operate the Audio and Visual Equipment in the Tutorial Room:

  1. Turn on the projector switch.
  2. Turn on the projector using the remote control.
  3. Turn on the lectern computer (if it's off).
  4. Turn on the Video Switch. The orange indicator now turns green. Select Channel 1 as the desired input. The feed from the Lectern PC is now routed to the projector.
After watching the video, click 'Next' below to proceed. If you're already familiar with the audio and visual equipment, you can click 'Next' now to skip to the next page.
