[fa-filter fa-lg] Alternate Sharing Options

Active Learning Classrooms are also equipped with various sharing options that can enhance the teaching and learning experience.These options are listed below and will be detailed in this module.

  • Split Screen
  • Group Presentation Mode
  • BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

 

[fa-columns fa-lg] Split Screen

This options enables the user to share two windows simultaneously to all available displays. 

  1. Open Microsoft Powerpoint, PDF reader or any app of your choice. Click and drag the top of the app window to the right of the screen until half the screen is highlighted and drop it.
  2. Open the Whiteboard app (aka-Easy Interactive Tools) on the lectern computer and minimise the window. Click and drag the top of the app window to the left of the screen until half the screen is highlighted and drop it. The writing pad may now be used to annotate on the whiteboard app. The document camera (via the windows camera app) may also be used instead of the whiteboard app.
    • Turn on the document camera.
    • Open the Windows Camera app on the lectern computer and minimise the window. Click and drag the top of the app window to the left of the screen until half the screen is highlighted and drop it.
    • Click the switch camera button if the document camera doesn't appear on screen by default.

 

[fa-users fa-lg] Group Presentation Mode

This option enables a student to physically connect their device to any available TV within the ALC. It provides the lectern user the ability to share the students presentation with all available displays.

  1. Click TV’s from the main menu and the options screen become available. The TV’s are numbered and labelled in each ALC.
  2. Choose the input TV display. If the Lectern user wishes to stream from TV number 1, click TV1. If the lectern user wishes to stream from TV number 2, click TV2. This facility allows a different device to be connected to each TV but the lectern user has the ability to choose which device is being shared to the overall class.

 

[fa-tablet fa-lg] BYOD - Fliptop

The ALC are equipped with a variety of inputs. This allows you to use your personal devices for teaching. Available inputs:

    • HDMI
    • VGA
    • Displayport
    • 3.5mm Audio jack
    • Usb extension to the lectern computer (usually for thumb drives)

Power sockets are also available in the fliptop interface. To retract any of the fliptop cables, press the illuminated button beside the cable.

Here is how you plug your device according to each input:

  1. Identify the VGA display cable and the 3.5mm audio jack.
  2. Connect the VGA display cable to the VGA port of your computer. A similar approach may be taken with other available display inputs such as the HDMI or display port interface. If your device is equipped with a Type C port, a Type C to VGA/HDMI adapter will be required.
  3. Connect the 3.5mm audio jack to your device. Note that this is only needed if the VGA connection is being used.
  4. Click the keys on your device to open the windows projection-menu. Then select from the four options presented to you.

Now that your device has been successfully connected, let's discuss the various display switching options available to you. Click on the fliptop option on the home screen. Here you may choose to do the following:

    • Share your device to just the TVs by clicking Share to All TVs.
    • Share your device to just the Projectors by clicking Share to All Projectors.
    • Share your device to all available displays by clicking Share to all.

Keep in mind that the Share to All TVs and Share to All Projectors option will no longer be available once the Share to All option has been selected. To make the Share to All TVs and Share to All Projectors options available once again, click Lectern PC on the home screen followed by Share to All TVs. Then repeat the steps above based on your shared device preference.

     

    [fa-barcode fa-lg] BYOD - Barco

    NOTE: This mode is currently only available in the following rooms: 6315, 6002 & 6003.

    This option enables the user to wirelessly stream their portable devices (smartphones, tablets and laptops) to the various displays available.

    1. Download the BARCO ClickShare application from the Apple App Store or the Google Play Store. If you are using a Laptop, download the app from the Barco Website using the link.
    2. Click on the Barco option on the home screen. Here you may choose to do the following:
      • Share your device to just the TVs by clicking Share to All TVs.
      • Share your device to just the Projectors by clicking Share to All Projectors.
      • Share your device to all available displays by clicking Share to all.
      • Keep in mind that the Share to All TVs and Share to All Projectors option will no longer be available once the Share to All option has been selected. To make the Share to All TVs and Share to All Projectors options available once again, click Lectern PC on the home screen followed by Share to All TVs. Then repeat the steps above based on your shared device preference.
    3. If you are using a smartphone or tablet, open the Barco Clickshare app. 
      • Key in the IP address shown on the bottom left of the projector screen/TV. 
      • Once completed, a password will be shown on the top right of the projector screen/TV. Key in the password. 
      • Click on the share button and then the start broadcast button. Your device will be mirrored wirelessly on the displays available in the ALC.
    4. If you are using a laptop, launch the Barco Clickshare program. 
      • Key in the IP address shown on the bottom left of the projector screen/TV and click enter. 
      • Once completed, a password will be shown on the top right of the projector screen/TV. Key in the password and click enter. 
      • Click on the share button and  your device will be mirrored wirelessly on the displays available in the ALC.
