To operate the Audio and Visual Equipment in the Active Learning Classroom:

  1. Turn on the Lectern computer if it's switched off.
  2. Start the ALC AV system.
    • Click Advance Controls to individually turn off any of the display devices. Click done to return to the previous menu.
    • Click Audio Setting to control the microphone volume levels. Click done to return to the previous menu.
    • Click on Light Controls to control the lighting in the ALC. The Lecture Mode preset turns off the lights in front of the projector screens for a clearer projection.
  3. Turn on the microphone. Press and hold the power button until the green indicator lights up. The ALC is equipped with  handheld microphones as well as a clip-on microphone. Always remember to turn off the microphones after use.
