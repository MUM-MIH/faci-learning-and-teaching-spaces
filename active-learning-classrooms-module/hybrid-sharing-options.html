[fa-share-alt-square fa-lg] Hybrid Sharing Options

ALC’s are able to stream the Lectern PC onto the projector screens while simultaneously streaming another device (connected by the fliptop interface) to the TV’s and vice versa. Here is how to configure this option.

Note: This is an Advanced Module. Do not hesitate to rewatch the video to understand the functionality of this Hybrid Sharing Option.

MIMO (Multiple Input Multiple Output)

The Hybrid sharing option, MIMO (Multiple Input Multiple Output) can be set up in the following configurations.
  • Fliptop - lectern combination
  • Barco - lectern combination
  • Fliptop - Barco combination

  1. Connect your own device to the Fliptop or Barco interface.
  2. Click on the Fliptop' or BARCO option (depending on step 1) on the home screen. Click Share to All TV’s or Share to All Projectors.
  3. Click Lectern PCon the home screen. Now depending on the Share to option previously chosen in step 2, choose the other option.
