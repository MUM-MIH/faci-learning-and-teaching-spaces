[fa-object-group fa-lg] Combined Mode

6603 and 6605 can be combined to form a larger classroom. In combined mode, a roomâ€™s lectern can be activated as the master lectern which allows the user to control the AV equipment in both rooms. Here is how to configure this option.

  1. Open the Crestron Application on the IPad Home screen. The room shown in the video is 6603 (Next Gen Lab 3).
  2. Tap Room Selection from the toolbar. Key in the combined mode password followed by Enter. Then tap Combined to enable combined mode.
  3. Tap Projector on the toolbar. The projector and screens for both labs can be controlled here. Watch the video to see the options available.
    • Tap Volume on the toolbar. The microphone and lectern audio levels for both labs can be controlled here. Watch the video to see the options available.
    • Tap Share on the toolbar. The source selections for both audio and video are available here. Watch the video to see the options available. The options can also be done using the Lab 5 control panel if it was accessed as the master lectern.
    • Tap Room Selection from the toolbar. Key in the combined mode password, located as a sticker on the IPad, followed by Enter. Then click Individual to disable combined mode. This should always be done after the end of a combined session.
