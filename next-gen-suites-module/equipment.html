To operate the Audio and Visual Equipment in the Next Gen Suite:

  1. Turn on the Lectern computer if it's switched off.
  2. Unlock the IPad with the provided password.
  3. Open the Crestron Application.
  4. Tap Projector on the toolbar. 
  5. Tap On to switch on the projector. The projector has to be turned on for the suiteâ€™s audio system to function.
  6. Tap Down to lower the projector screen.
  7. Tap Desktop to select the lectern computer as the audio and video input.
  8. Turn on the Microphone.
  9. Tap Volume on the toolbar. Here you can control the microphone and computer audio levels.
