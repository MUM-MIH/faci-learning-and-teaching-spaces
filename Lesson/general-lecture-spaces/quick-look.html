[fa-audio-description fa-lg] Audio & Visual Equipment

To operate the Audio and Visual Equipment in the General Lecture Spaces:

  1. Turn on the projector using the remote control.
  2. Turn on the Lectern computer if it's switched off.
  3. Select Channel 1 as the desired output. The feed from the lectern computer is now routed to the projector.
  4. There are three types of microphones used in the general lecture spaces:
    • Lectern Microphone
    • Handheld Microphone
    • Clip On Microphone
  5. To turn on the microphone, ensure that the green indicator is lit when using the device. Press the mute button to mute the microphone. The green indicator now turns off.
