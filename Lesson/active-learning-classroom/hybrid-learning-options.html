[fa-share-alt-square fa-lg] Hybrid Sharing Options

Did you know that the Lectern PC and the BYOD option can be used together to deliver a unique teaching and learning experience?
ALC’s are able to stream the lectern pc onto the projector screens while simultaneously streaming another device (connected by the fliptop interface) to the TV’s and vice versa. Here is how to configure this option.

Hybrid Sharing Option
M I M O (Multiple Input Multiple Output)
This option enables the user to stream from multiple devices to multiple displays to deliver a unique teaching and learning experience.

  1. Connect your own device to the fliptop/Barco interface.
  2. Click on the fliptop option on the home screen. Click “Share to All TV’s” or “Share to All Projectors”.
  3. Click “Lectern computer” on the home screen. Now depending on the option previously chosen in Step 2, choose the other option.

    Similarly, the hybrid sharing option can be repeated with the Lectern computer and Barco combination, or even the Fliptop and Barco combination depending on your needs.
