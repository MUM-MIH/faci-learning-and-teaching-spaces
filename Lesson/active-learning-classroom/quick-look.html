To operate the Audio and Visual Equipment in the Active Learning Classroom:

  1. Turn on the projector using the remote control.
  2. Start the ALC AV system.
    • To individually turn off any of the display devices, click advance controls and this menu will appear. Click done to return to the previous menu.
    • To control the microphone volumes, click audio setting and this menu will appear.Use the first slider to control the volume of Handheld Mic 1. To mute the microphone, click the mute button.
      The same can be done for Handheld Mic 2. Use the slider to control the volume of the Clip microphone. To mute the microphone, click the mute button.
      The final slider is a Master volume control. This mute button will mute all the microphones simultaneously. Click done to return to the previous menu.
    • To individually turn off any of the display devices, click advance controls and this menu will appear. Click done to return to the previous menu.
    • To control the lighting, click on Light Controls and select from the available presets. Take note that the Lecture Mode preset turns off the lights in front of the projector screens to facilitate a clearer projection.
  3. Microphone Operation Here are the three types of microphones used in the general lecture spaces
    Lectern Microphone
    Handheld Microphone
    Clip On Microphone

    These devices are subject to availability.
  4. This is the lectern microphone. Ensure that the green indicator is lit when using the device. Press the mute button to mute the microphone.
    The green indicator now turns off.
  5. This is the handheld microphone. To utilize this microphone, turn on the device. The green indicator will light up.
    Turn off the microphone after use.
  6. This is the clip on the microphone. To utilize this microphone, turn on the device. The green indicator will light up.
    Turn off the microphone after use.
