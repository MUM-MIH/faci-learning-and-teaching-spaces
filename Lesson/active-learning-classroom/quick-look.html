To operate the Audio and Visual Equipment in the Active Learning Classroom:

  1. Turn on the projector using the remote control.
  2. Start the ALC AV system.
    • To individually turn off any of the display devices, click advance controls and a menu will appear. Click done to return to the previous menu.
    • To control the microphone volumes, click audio setting and this menu will appear. Use the first slider to control the volume of Handheld Mic 1. To mute the microphone, click the mute button. The same applies to Handheld Mic 2. Use the slider to control the volume of the Clip microphone. To mute the microphone, click the mute button.
      The final slider is a Master volume control. This mute button will mute all the microphones simultaneously. Click done to return to the previous menu.
    • To control the lighting, click on Light Controls and select from the available presets. Take note that the Lecture Mode preset turns off the lights in front of the projector screens to facilitate a clearer projection.
  3. There are three types of microphones used in the general lecture spaces:
    • Lectern Microphone
    • Handheld Microphone
    • Clip On Microphone
  4. To turn on the microphone, ensure that the green indicator is lit when using the device. Press the mute button to mute the microphone. The green indicator now turns off.
