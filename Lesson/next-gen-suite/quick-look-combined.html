[fa-object-group fa-lg] Combined Mode

6603 and 6605 can be combined to form a larger classroom. In combined mode, a room’s lectern can be activated as the master lectern which allows the user to control the AV equipment in both rooms. Here is how to configure this option.

  1. Tap “Crestron” on the Ipad Home screen. In this video, we have chosen 6603 (also known as Next Gen lab 3) to demonstrate this feature.
  2. Tap “Room Selection” from the toolbar. Key in the combined mode password followed by “Enter”. Then tap “Combined” to enable combined mode.
  3. Tap “Projector” on the toolbar. Here you can choose to do the following.
    • Control Projector 1 in Lab 3.
    • Control Projector 2 in Lab 3.
    • Control Screen 1 in Lab 3.
    • Control Screen 2 in Lab 3.
    • Control Projector 2 in Lab 3.
    • Control Screen 2 in Lab 3.
    • Control the Projector in Lab 5.
    • Control the Screen in Lab 5.
  4. Tap “Volume” on the toolbar. Here you can choose to do the following.
    • Control the microphone level in Lab 3.
    • Control the microphone level in Lab 5.
    • Control the lectern audio level in Lab 3.
    • Control the lectern audio level in Lab 5.
  5. Tap “Share” on the toolbar. Here you can choose to do the following.
    • Stream the Lectern computer in Lab 3 to the projectors in both labs.
    • Stream your own device connected in Lab 3 via VGA to the projectors in both labs.
    • Stream your own device connected in Lab 3 via HDMI to the projectors in both labs.

    The options mentioned can also be done using the Lab 5 control panel.

  6. The user can also stream in various other configurations depending on their needs.
    • Stream the Lectern computer in Lab 3 to the projectors in Lab 3 and stream the Lectern computer in Lab 5 to the projector in Lab 5.
    • Stream the Lectern computer in Lab 3 to the projectors in Lab 3 and share your own device connected in Lab 5 via VGA to the projector Lab 5.
    • Stream the Lectern computer in Lab 3 to the projectors in Lab 3 and stream your own device connected in Lab 5 via HDMI to the projector in Lab 5.
    • Stream your own device connected in Lab 3 via VGA to the projectors in Lab 3 and stream your own device connected in Lab 5 via HDMI to the projector in Lab 5.
    • Stream your own device connected in Lab 3 via HDMI to the projectors in Lab 3 and stream your own device connected in Lab 5 via HDMI to the projector in Lab 5.
  7. Tap “Room Selection” from the toolbar. Key in the combined mode password followed by “Enter”. Then click “Individual” to disable combined mode. This should always be done after the end of a combined session.
