[fa-tablet fa-lg] BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

TThe Next Gen Suites are equipped with a variety of inputs. This allows you to use your personal devices for teaching.

  • HDMI
  • Audio jack 3.5mm
  • VGA
  • Displayport
  • Usb Interface Image

  1. Identify the VGA display cable and the 3.5mm audio jack.
  2. Connect the VGA display cable to the VGA port of your computer. A similar approach may be taken with other available display inputs such as HDMI or display port.
    • If your device is equipped with a Type C port, a Type C to VGA/HDMI adapter will be required.
  3. Connect the 3.5mm audio jack to your device. This is only needed if the VGA connection is being used.
  4. Tap ”Desktop” as the video/audio source.
  5. Press the keys on your device to open the windows projection menu. Then select from the four options presented to you.
