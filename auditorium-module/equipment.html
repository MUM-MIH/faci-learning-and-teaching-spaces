To operate the Audio and Visual Equipment in the Auditorium:

  1. Turn on the Lectern computer if it's switched off.
  2. Unlock the Lectern with the provided password.
  3. Open the Crestron Application located on the IPad home screen.
  4. Select Projector on the toolbar. Click ON to switch on one or either both projectors.
  5. Select Presentation on the toolbar. Select your desired input and outputs.
  6. Select PC to select the lectern computer as the desired input and then select Projector 1 as the output. The video feed from the lectern computer is now displayed through Projector 1.
    • To stream both projectors, repeat Step 6 for Projector 2.
  7. Select PC to select the lectern computer as the desired input and then select Audio as the output. The audio feed from the lectern computer is now routed to the Auditoriumâ€™s speakers.
  8. There are two types of microphones used in Auditorium.
    • Lectern Microphone
    • Clip On Microphone
  9. (Lectern Microphone ONLY) To turn on the microphone, ensure that the green indicator is lit when using the device. Press the mute button to mute the microphone. The green indicator now turns off.
  10. To adjust the audio levels in the Auditorium, use the arrow keys to increase and decrease the audio output. The arrow keys control the master audio in the Auditorium. To mute the audio, tap the mute button.
  11. Select Lighting to bring up the Auditorium's lighting controls. There are a few available presets to choose from (shown in the video).
